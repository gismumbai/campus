About Us

A complete educational environment with academic discipline and student guidance.

Global Degree College, Bhiwandi works with a student-first approach, focusing on academic learning, discipline, digital awareness, employability skills, and holistic growth. This website structure is designed to present the institution professionally for parents, students, teachers, university visitors, and NAAC/IQAC purposes.

1
Vision

To build a progressive educational environment that supports quality learning, values, skills, and responsible citizenship.

2

To provide accessible, disciplined, technology-supported, and career-oriented education for students from diverse backgrounds.

To maintain documentation, feedback, student support, academic planning, and continuous improvement practices.
